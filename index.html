Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Welcome to BlackScan! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Quick Summary: BlackScan helps you discover Black-owned businesses and products by scanning items with your camera. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service and never sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple ID, we receive basic profile information such as your name and email address (if you choose to share it).
  • Profile Data: Any additional information you choose to add to your profile.
  • Saved Items: Products and companies you save or add to your cart.
  • Feedback: Any feedback, ratings, or reviews you submit through the app.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Data: When you scan products, we process images locally on your device to identify products. These images are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them.
  • Scan History: Records of products you've scanned (stored locally on your device).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as features used, search queries, and pages visited.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Analytics Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and usage statistics to improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your product scans and deliver search results
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant product recommendations
  • Save your preferences, favorites, and shopping cart
  • Send you important updates about the app (with your permission)
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security concerns
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Stored securely using enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure
  • Access-controlled with strict authentication requirements
  • Regularly backed up to prevent data loss

Local Storage: Some data (such as scan history and saved items) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our app:

4.1 Authentication

  • Sign in with Apple: For secure user authentication. Apple's Privacy Policy applies.

4.2 Data Storage and Search

  • Typesense: For fast product search functionality
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and backend services

4.3 Analytics

  • Analytics Services: We may use analytics services to understand app usage and improve performance. These services collect anonymous usage data.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information. We recommend reviewing their policies.

5. Camera and Photo Library Access

BlackScan requires camera access to scan products. We use a combination of on-device and cloud-based processing to identify products:

5.1 On-Device Processing

  • Initial text recognition (OCR) is performed locally on your device
  • When on-device analysis produces high-quality results, no images leave your device

5.2 Cloud-Based Analysis

  • When on-device OCR quality is insufficient, product images may be sent to OpenAI's API for advanced analysis using their GPT-4 Vision technology
  • Images are transmitted securely over HTTPS and are processed by OpenAI in accordance with their API data usage policy
  • OpenAI does not use API inputs to train their models
  • Images are not stored permanently by BlackScan or OpenAI after processing

5.3 Your Controls

  • You can revoke camera access at any time through your device settings, though this will limit core scanning functionality
  • No images are saved to your device or our servers after scanning is complete

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Business Transfers: If BlackScan is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you personally

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the rights mentioned in Section 7. Our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for specific processing purposes
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to perform our services
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is required by law
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is in our legitimate interests and doesn't override your rights
